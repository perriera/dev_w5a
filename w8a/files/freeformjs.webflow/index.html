How to use FreeFormJS out of the box:

You can use FreeFormJS straight out of the box. That is, you do not need to do anything special other than download your Webflow zip file into your Downloads directory and from there, drag the downloaded Webflow file into the box you see above. From there simply follow the directions.

Prerequisite #1: Webflow

You will need an account with Webflow.com.

Prerequisite #2: Code Export

You will need a non-free Webflow account in order to use FreeFormJS as you need the ability to be able to export your Webflow project into your downloads folder. Presently, Webflow offers their Webflow Lite package which will allow you do a Code Export.

Prerequisite #3: Modern Browser

You will need to use the latest browsers available on the Internet today. Either Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari or Google Chrome will do just fine, they just have to be the newest versions available. This website will not work with versions of Firefox or IE that are more than two years old.

Prerequisite #4: The FreeFormJS Five for Free Daily Plan

At present FreeFormJS will allow you to use the service for free but in a limited manner. Such that registered users that do not have a subscription can have five free conversions a day. That's enough to get your feet wet, however, serious developers are encouraged to purchase a subscription plan. (See Subscriptions under Profile)

Prerequisite #5: PayPal

This site makes exclusive use of PayPal. If you want to purchase a subscription you will have to setup a PayPal account.

How to cancel your subscription

If you cancel your membership access to membership features will expire at the end of the billing cycle. You will still have access to the features of this site till then.
  1. Log into the PayPal website: https://www.paypal.com
  2. Click Profile
  3. Click Profile & Settings
  4. Click My money
  5. Click My preapproved payments
  6. Click Update
  7. Look for FreeFormJS in the listing and click on it
  8. Click Cancel
PayPal.com requires that you visit your site to cancel your subscription manually. This ensures that you subscription is indeed cancelled. As much as we would like to cancel it for you at the press of a button, PayPal policies dictate that you have to do the above to cancel subscription based memberships.
